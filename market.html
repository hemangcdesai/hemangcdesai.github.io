Target Sectors

Where TalentScoring Operates

The résuméless workforce spans nearly every sector of India's physical economy. These are TalentScoring's primary markets.

🏗️

Construction

India's construction sector employs tens of millions of daily wage workers — many hired through unstructured, informal means.

🏭

Manufacturing

Factory floors require reliable, consistent workers. Screening for attendance, reliability, and fit is critical — and currently ad hoc.

📦

Logistics & E-Commerce

India's delivery economy is growing at pace. Consistent screening for delivery riders and warehouse workers is a clear need.

🚗

Transportation

Drivers and fleet operators need reliable, accountable hires. Voice screening can assess communication, availability, and work history.

🍽️

Hospitality

Hotels, restaurants, and catering operations need workers who are reliable and client-facing — traits that voice screening can reveal.

🏠

Domestic Services

Cooks, cleaners, security guards, and other home staff — among the most trust-sensitive and least screened hires in India.
