The Solution

If They Can't Write a Résumé, They Can Still Make a Phone Call

TalentScoring uses voice-based AI screening in native languages — accessible to every worker, affordable for every employer.

How It Works

Four Steps to a Better Hire

No app. No résumé. No barrier. Just an incoming phone call that gives employers the insight they need.

1

Job is Advertised

Instead of an email address, the job ad displays a telephone number for applicants to call.

2

Candidate Calls

The worker calls in their native language — no smartphone, no email, no document required.

3

AI Screens

Pre-scripted questions are asked. Responses are scored for performance, accountability, and retention potential.

4

Best Candidates Surface

Hiring managers only meet those who pass screening. Every minute is spent on qualified candidates.

Native Language First

Screening in the Language Workers Actually Speak

No electrician, cook, or delivery rider finds a conversation in their native language invasive. It's the most natural format possible.

TalentScoring's question sets are designed for real-world blue-collar contexts — probing for work history, family situation, reliability signals, and accountability indicators through structured, conversational prompts.

Follow-up questions adapt dynamically based on earlier responses, giving a richer picture of each candidate without adding friction.

Sample screening questions
क्या आपने पहले कभी काम किया है?
Have you worked before?
घर पे कौन कौन है?
Who is at home? (Family situation)
आखिरी नौकरी क्यों छोड़ी?
Why did you leave your last job?
आप कब से काम शुरू कर सकते हैं?
When can you start work?
