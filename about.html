Our Origin

Two Products. One Mission.

ShortestPath Private Limited was founded to make talent markets more efficient — not just for employers and sophisticated knowledge workers, but for every Indian who wants to work.

CareerNodes, launched in early 2025, helps candidates structure their experience, become more competitive, and navigate the ATS-gated job market. Within 15 months it has grown to 2,000+ paying customers across 29 Indian states, without VC funding.

But building CareerNodes taught us something important: résumés only tell less than a third of the story. India's largest workforce — 250 million strong — doesn't use résumés at all. And the tools to screen them simply don't exist.

TalentScoring is our answer to the overwhelming majority.
